How to get to id KAIST

[ Airport Limousine Bus ]

We recommend 2 main ways from Two Int'l Airport to KAIST
by Airpot Limousine bus and Express Train(KTX). The picture and explanation below will help your comfortable travel



1. Airport to Daejeon Government Complex

1) Incheon Int'l Airport - Daejeon Government Complex
You can buy the limousine bus tickets for Daejeon Government Complex at the Transportation Information Counters (near the exits 4, 6, 7, 8, 11, 13 on the 1st floor). With the bus ticket, you should go to 9D (The bus stop for Daejeon Government Complex) on the 1st floor to get on the bus. The bus runs every 30 minutes from 06:00 to 23:10 and it will take you about 2 hours 30 minutes~3 hours and cost you KRW 23,100 as of January, 2013.

2) Gimpo Int'l Airport - Daejeon Government Complex
You can buy the limousine bus tickets for Daejeon Government Complex at the 2nd floor. With the bus ticket, you should go to gate 3. The bus runs 7:25, 10:55, 11:55, 13:55, 14:55, 16:55, 18:45, and 20:25 and it will take you about 150~180 minutes and cost you KRW 14,300 as of January, 2013.

2. Daejeon Government Complex to KAIST(Venue)

We strongly recommend that you take a taxi to get to KAIST upon arrival at Daejeon Government Complex. It should not be difficult to get a taxi around Bus Terminal. Please tell the driver your destination (KAIST, Industrial Design Building B/D No. #N25 / In Korean, KAIST SanEop Design Hakkua Building). It will take about 10 minutes and cost you about KRW 5,000.

1. Airport to Seoul Station


1) Incheon Int'l Airport - [Subway] - Seoul Station
You can buy the Airport Railroad tickets for Seoul Station at the Airport Railroad (AREX) Incheon Intâ€™l Airport Station (B1 Floor). The AREX runs at the average interval of 12 minutes from 05:20 to 24:00 and it will take about 50 minutes and cost you 4,050 won as of January, 2013. And the deposit is 500 won.
2) Gimpo Int'l Airport - [Subway] - Seoul Station
The Airport Express Line passes through Gimpo Int'l Airport and it will take about 20 minutes.

2. Seoul Station to Daejeon Station

At Seoul Station, you can take KTX (Korean Train Express), which is the fastest train in Korea. It runs at the average speed of 300km per hour and will comfortably take you to Daejeon Station within an hour. Please note that its fare is relatively higher than that of Airport Limousine bus. 3. Daejeon Station to KAIST(Venue)

We strongly recommend that you take a taxi to get to hotels or KAIST upon arrival at Daejeon Station. It should not be difficult to get a taxi at Daejeon Station. Please tell the driver your destination (KAIST, Industrial Design Building B/D No. #N25 / In Korean, KAIST SanEop Design Hakkua Building).
